About Finance Tracker

Finance Tracker is a comprehensive personal finance management application that helps you track your daily expenses, analyze spending patterns, and make informed financial decisions.

Features:

  • Track expenses with detailed categorization
  • Support for multiple payment methods
  • Interactive charts and analytics
  • Monthly and yearly summaries
  • Data import/export functionality
  • Responsive design for all devices

Version: 1.0.0
Data Storage: Local Storage (Client-side)
